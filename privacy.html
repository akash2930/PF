Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's exactly what we collect (very little), what we don't collect (your prompts), and how we keep things clean.

🔒 Privacy at a glance

  • ✓ We do not store the prompts you generate.
  • ✓ We do not require account creation or personal information to use the tool.
  • ✓ We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.
  • ✓ API requests are forwarded to Groq and subject to Groq's own privacy policy.

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Who We Are

PromptForge ("we", "us", "our") is a free, client-side AI prompt generation tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website and use the PromptForge generator.

2. Information We Collect

2a. Information you provide

When you use the Contact form, we receive your name, email address, and message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is never shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

2b. Usage data

Like most websites, our server logs may automatically collect standard web log data including your IP address, browser type, referring URL, and the pages you visit. This data is used for security monitoring and aggregate analytics only, and is retained for no longer than 30 days.

2c. What we do NOT collect

We do not store, log, or retain the content of the prompts you generate, the topics you enter, or any form data submitted to the AI generator. That information is passed directly to the Groq API and is not stored on our systems.

3. Third-Party Services

Groq API

When you generate a prompt, your input is transmitted to Groq's API endpoints (api.groq.com). Groq processes this data to generate a response. Please review Groq's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle API data.

Tailwind CSS CDN

We load Tailwind CSS from the official Tailwind CDN. This may result in a standard CDN request log entry at Tailwind's infrastructure provider.

Google Fonts

Typography is loaded from Google Fonts. Google may log the font request. See Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

PromptForge does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. We do not set any persistent cookies. Session storage may be used temporarily for UI state (e.g., previously selected form options) and is cleared when you close your browser tab.

5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions may be retained for up to 12 months to manage support queries. Web server logs are retained for up to 30 days. No other user data is retained.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us via the Contact page.

7. Children's Privacy

PromptForge is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of PromptForge after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact page.
